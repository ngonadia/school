BRIEF HISTORY

Grace Academy nnewichi, founded in September 2024, is the brain-child of the Proprietor, Mr Chigozie Onwugbenu (Tokumbo), whose passion for education, godliness and moral excellence has been the driving force for this major investment in the education industry. In a world where our youths are growing up to be plagued with the "get-rich-quick" syndrome, and academic and moral absolutes are snubbed, Mr Chigozie Onwugbenu is committed to helping our children discover their purpose and moral direction in life. He is particularly concerned about grooming a new generation of future leaders with deep convictions and sound values that are rooted in God's word and hardwork.

CONCEPTION OF GRACE ACADEMY

Grace Academy was born out of the love and passion of Mr Chigozie Onwugbenu for education in memory and honour of his beloved mother, Mrs Grace Onwugbenu, who was a passionate advocate of good children up bringing and hard work. As can be easily noticed today, not all parents are parents with the mind set for transformative and responsible up bringing of children. A peep into the structure and manner of trending parenting, you will discover that majority of parents are devoid of the fear of God in the early child up bringing. This is a major contributor to faulty leadership in all the sectors of the economy in the nation. Mrs Grace Onwugbenu believed that to change the future, we must begin early by shaping the early years of our children at home and school respectively. This philosophy means, if you want tommorow to be good, you must impact positively on today's child who will in turn, effect positive change tomorrow. That is the motivation that powered the conception of Grace Academy.

THE PHILOSOPHY OF GRACE ACADEMY

Grace Academy is a school committed to an all round qualitative education with emphasis on academic and moral excellence. The school is making serious arrangement to invest massively in modern learning technology and has assembled competent and committed teaching and administrative personnel, as well as a conducive environment. Grace Academy is committed to providing the highest academic standards, as well as enriching lives through spiritual development. Small class size concept is adopted with it's main benefits, including the individualized attention and care, in addition to teacher accessibility. The aim is to produce critical thinking and God minded children who will positively, impact the larger society. Grace Academy operates a curricular programme which focuses on congintive, affective and psychomotor domains of the learners.

OBJECTIVES OF GRACE ACADEMY

Since the society is becoming complex by the day, the education of children of our nation calls for a dynamic approach to arrest the ugly trend of "get-rich-quick" syndrome and give the children the kind of education that makes for a sound, spiritual, moral, educational and total development. This is the only way to guarantee a sound future and hope for the nation.
