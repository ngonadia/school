VISION STATEMENT

To be a leading educational institution, renowned for its holistic approach to education, innovative teaching methods, and commitment to excellence.

MISSION STATEMENT

Grace Academy is committed to providing a nurturing and enabling environment that fosters academic excellence, character development and spiritual growth, so as to produce future leaders that will excel in an ever-changing world.
